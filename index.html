jquery.cookiefy

It´s a very lightweight, adjustable and easy-to-use plugin for the EU-law cookie warning.

jquery.cookiefy on GitHub

What is jquery.cookiefy?

Almost every website is using cookies. If you are also using cookies on your site, you have to provide information for your visitors and inform them about your usage of cookies. jquery.cookiefy simplifies this process because you only have to adust the text and the CSS to your custom needs if you´d like and you are ready to go.jquery.cookiefy is a lightweight jquery-plugin that helps you, if you have to create a cookie warning in your site according to the EU-cookie-law.
